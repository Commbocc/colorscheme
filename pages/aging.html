What Services Do I Qualify For?

Aging Services

Hillsborough County Aging Services offers a myriad of services designed to enhance the lives of older adults age 50 and over. For active adult programming, in-home services, a hot meal, and referral services, Aging Services is your one-stop location for older adult services.

The mission of the Department of Aging Services is to coordinate essential health and wellness services for older adults to encourage maximum independence and prosperity with an emphasis on the seven dimensions of wellness: physical, emotional, intellectual, social, spiritual, environmental, and financial.
